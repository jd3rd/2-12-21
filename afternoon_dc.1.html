How to Spend an Afternoon in Washington, DC

This page should give you some great ways to spend a few hours in Washington, DC. Whether you are travelling alone, with a friend, or bringing the entire family, these stops provide a great experience. There are different ways to get around town and you should check weekend schedule on Metro prior to deciding whether you would like to take Metro Rail or Bus. You can always drive or take an Uber. Once you are downtown, walking from one destination to another is very easy. For information about specific exhibits, visit the museum web sites by clicking on one of the links below. Enjoy your stay!
